2. About Bunnie

Bunnie is an AI-powered daily planner and task management application that helps users:

  • Schedule and manage tasks
  • Record meetings and audio
  • Convert recordings into notes and tasks
  • Upload documents and files for summaries and Q&A

The App requires account creation using an email address and password.

  • Support communications

B. Information Collected Automatically

  • Device type, OS version, app version
  • IP address and approximate location
